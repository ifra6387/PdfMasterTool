Why Work With Us?

At I Love Making PDF, we're passionate about creating tools that make a real difference in people's daily work. Join a team that values innovation, collaboration, and the pursuit of excellence in everything we do.

Our Values

We believe in building a workplace where creativity thrives, diversity is celebrated, and every team member has the opportunity to grow and make an impact.

What We Offer

  • Competitive Compensation: Fair salaries with performance-based bonuses
  • Flexible Work: Remote-first culture with flexible hours
  • Health Benefits: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
  • Professional Growth: Learning budgets and conference attendance
  • Innovation Time: 20% time for personal projects and experimentation
  • Collaborative Environment: Work with talented professionals across the globe

Open Positions

Frontend Developer

We're looking for a skilled Frontend Developer to help build intuitive user interfaces for our PDF tools. Experience with React, TypeScript, and modern CSS frameworks preferred.

Backend Engineer

Join our backend team to develop scalable APIs and document processing systems. Experience with Node.js, Python, and cloud technologies is a plus.

Product Designer

Help design user experiences that make complex PDF operations simple and accessible. We value designers who understand both aesthetics and usability.

DevOps Engineer

Build and maintain the infrastructure that powers millions of PDF operations daily. Experience with containerization, CI/CD, and monitoring systems preferred.

Quality Assurance Engineer

Ensure our tools work flawlessly across different platforms and use cases. We're looking for detail-oriented professionals who are passionate about quality.
